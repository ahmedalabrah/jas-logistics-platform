MASTER LOGISTICS AGREEMENT

BETWEEN

JAS LOGISTICS SOLUTIONS

AND

GLOBAL ALLIANCE CORP

1. DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATIONS

1.1 "Services" shall mean the logistics, freight forwarding, and supply chain management solutions provided by JAS Logistics as detailed in Appendix A.

1.2 "Term" means the period commencing on the Effective Date and continuing for twenty-four (24) months, unless terminated earlier in accordance with Section 14.

2. OPERATIONAL SCOPE

The Provider agrees to maintain a 98.5% on-time delivery metric for all cross-border transits within the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) region. Failure to meet these metrics for three consecutive quarters will trigger a service review protocol.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

  • Transit Time Accuracy: ± 4 hours
  • Cargo Damage Ratio: < 0.02%
  • Documentation Compliance: 100%

3. FINANCIAL TERMS

All invoicing shall be processed via the JAS Command Center platform. Payment terms are Net-30 from the date of digital receipt. Late payments shall accrue interest at a rate of 1.5% per month.
