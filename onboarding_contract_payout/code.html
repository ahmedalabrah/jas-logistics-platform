Carrier Master Services Agreement

1. Scope of Services

The Second Party (Carrier) agrees to provide logistical transport and delivery services across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia in accordance with the TGA (Transport General Authority) regulations and JAS operational standards.

verified 2. Commission Structure

JAS shall retain a standard commission of 12.5% on each successful shipment delivery. Carrier payout is calculated net of VAT and local regulatory fees.

3. Payment Cycle

Payouts will be processed to the nominated IBAN within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Cycle is determined by selection in Section 5.4 of the digital onboarding portal.

payments 4. COD (Cash On Delivery)

Carrier is responsible for the collection and safe remittance of COD amounts. Remittance to JAS must occur within 24 hours of collection via the JAS Smart-Drop system.

5. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with a 30-day written notice. Instant termination applies for breach of TGA licensing or safety protocols.
