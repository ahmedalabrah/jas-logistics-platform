MASTER FRAMEWORK AGREEMENT

Ref: JAS-SOV-2024-FA88

1. DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION

1.1 In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires, the following expressions shall have the following meanings: "Services" means the global logistics, freight forwarding, and warehousing services provided by JAS Global.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

2.1 The Service Provider agrees to provide the Customer with logistics services across the EMEA region as detailed in Annex A. The Customer commits to a minimum volume utilization of 60% of the projected quarterly capacity.

2.2 Priority handling shall be afforded to all "Gold Tier" shipments as identified in the digital booking manifest.

3. FINANCIAL TERMS

3.1 Pricing shall be based on the JAS Sovereign Dynamic Index (JSDI) as updated bi-weekly. Standard payment terms remain Net 30 from invoice issuance.
