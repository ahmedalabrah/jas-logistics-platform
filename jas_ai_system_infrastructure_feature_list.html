`predictive-maintenance`: Predictive Maintenance (FMS) - `gate-resolution`: Gate Fail Auto-Resolution (FMS) - `route-opt`: Dynamic Route Optimization (FMS) - `cold-chain-anomaly`: Cold Chain Anomaly Detection (FMS) - `fuel-fraud`: Fuel Fraud Detection (FMS) - `sc-coaching`: SC Performance Coaching (SCP) - `vehicle-retirement`: Vehicle Retirement Analysis (FMS) - `grn-damage`: GRN Damage Computer Vision (WMS) - `storage-allocation`: Smart Storage Allocation (WMS) - `volume-forecast`: Inbound Volume Forecasting (WMS) - `task-queue`: Intelligent Task Queue (WMS) - `expiry-risk`: Expiry Risk Intelligence (WMS) - `hs-classifier`: HS Code AI Classification (FreMS) - `customs-channel`: Customs Channel Prediction (FreMS) - `rate-advisor`: Freight Rate Timing Advisor (FreMS) - `doc-autofill`: Document Auto-fill (FreMS) - `route-optimizer`: Multimodal Route Optimizer (FreMS) - `supply-chain-assistant`: Supply Chain Assistant (Client) - `fa-planning`: FA Planning Assistant (Client) - `spend-optimizer`: Spend Optimizer (Client) - `reorder-intelligence`: Inventory Reorder Intelligence (Client) - `dispute-letter`: Invoice Dispute Intelligence (Client) - `sc-maintenance`: SC Predictive Maintenance (SCP) - `sc-earnings`: SC Earnings Opportunity (SCP) - `invoice-anomaly`: Invoice Anomaly Detection (Finance) - Arabic (SCP) ### Performance & Strategy - **Loading:** Lazy-loaded via React.lazy and Suspense. - **Caching:** Stale-while-revalidate (SWR) pattern with varying TTLs (5m to 4h). - **Prioritization:** Dispatch > Compliance > Finance > Coaching. - **Error Handling:** Non-blocking indigo error components with retry logic. ## Presentation Tooling - **Topbar Indicator:** Real-time AI health status (Online/Degraded/Offline). - **Demo Mode:** `?demo=ai` parameter to pre-populate all insights for presentations.
